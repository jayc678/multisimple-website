Topic: What is work,energy and power.

What is Work:

  • Work is the product of force and distance. Mathematically,work(w)=force(F)*
    distance(s).SI unit of work is Joules.

What is Energy:

  • Energy is the ability to do work.SI unit of energy is Joules.

Forms of energy

  1. Mechanical energy
  2. Solar energy
  3. Nuclear energy
  4. Gravitational potential energy
  5. Rest energy
  6. Electrical energy
  7. Sound energy

What is Power:

  • Power is the rate at which work is done. Mathematically, power(P)=work done(w)/time taken(t) or force(F)
    *velocity(v).SIunit of power is Watt.

We are going to treat on mechanical energy

Mechanical energy is the energy required by machines to be at rest or in motion

Types of mechanical energy

  1. Kinetic energy: Energy which is due to motion or energy required by an objects to start moving. Mathematically,KE=mv2/2
  2. Potential energy: Energy which is due to rest or energy required by objects to come to halt or to rest.PE=mgh
