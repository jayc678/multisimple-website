Questions:

  1. A boy of mass 20kg walked a distance of 2m.Find the work done by the boy?
  2. A man uses a force of 20N to pull a ball of mass 2kg at constant velocity.If the velocity attained is 5.5m/s.find power?.
  3. A ball of mass 20kg kicked by a boy with a force of 3N moved with a uniform speed of 10m/s in 5mins.
  4. a)List out all the parameters stated above.
    b)Calculate the distance covered by the ball
    c)Calculate the power.
  5. Calculate the work done in moving a box of mass 50kg through a distamce
    of 20m.(Take g=10m/s^2)
  6. A ball is projected to the air with velocity of 10m/s.If the mass of the
    ball is 20kg.Find the kinetic energy?

Workings:

ANS for Ques

  1. Mass=20kg,gravity=10,distance=2m
    Work=Mass*gravity*distance=20*10*2=400J

  2. F=20,V=5.5
    P=FV=20*5.5=110W

  3. M=20kg,V=10m/s,t=5mins-5*60=300secs,F=3N
  4. Since V=s/t then, s=tV
    s=5*60*10[remember that t is in mins so you convert it to secs ]
    s=3000m or 3km.
  5. P=FV
    P=3*10=30W
  6. W=fs
    W=mgs
    W=50*10*20=10000J
  7. KE=mv^2/2
    KE=20*10^2/2=2000/2=1000J
